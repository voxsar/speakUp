Do you have questions about depression?

Speak Up is a initiative by the Rotaract Club of College of Chemical Sciences to understand about depression

We live in a culture and society where people are reluctant and very much afraid to reach out and talk about their state of mental health. For reasons such as the taboo and social stigma associated with it, or simply because they don’t have access to or cannot afford the necessary professional help. As a solution to this widespread problem the Rotaract Club of CCS is launching “Speak Up”, an online campaign that allows anyone from anywhere in the world to submit their questions regarding mental health, anonymously.


We will be reaching out and directing these questions to professionals with expertise in the field of psychiatry in a completely safe environment.

Speak Up also aims to build a community made of post depression recoveries and support groups that would help people under going depression to cope with their conditions and to better understand themselves.

They will also be answered by the community or people whom have gone through similar experiences, the sharing of these experiences will help cope


The answers and solutions to these problem will be accessible to everyone so that it can benefit the people with similar issues. So head onto ‘website link here’ and leave any queries you may have in this regard.
